Energizing Humanity.

Welcome to our organization! In today's times, when our stores of fossil fuels are running out, and global warming is on the rise because of increasing amounts of greenhouse gases in the atmosphere, which are released on the combustion of fossil fuels, we aim to produce clean, renewable energy, and providing energy to rural areas at minimum costs and positively impact the living conditions.



We started as a small startup within India, but in the last decade,we have expanded as an organisation. Now we have 14 branches: 4 within India and the rest abroad. Our headquarters are at Mumbai, which is also the origin of our organisation.

Looking at some numbers...

We have worked over the last 10 years, and have also acheieved numerous milestones. Some of them are:

  1. We have produced approximately 48052 gigawatt hours since we started producing energy in December, 2020.
  2. We have produced nearly 46130 gigawatt hours in renewable ways, almost 96% of our total energy production. As a result, we have recieved the Clean&Green award by UNESCO in 2018.
  3. Our NGO has also thrived, funded by 50% of our profits, besides the generous contributions of investors and donations from the public.
